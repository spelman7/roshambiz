A game of Wikipedia clicking.

Use clues to click down a specific Wikipedia tunnel. Winners get a prize!

Instructions

1. Install this Chrome Extension.

2. Close all open Wikipedia sessions.

3. Click the Goose Chase Icon to open the Chrome Extension.

4. Open this page in a new tab.

5. Starting at that page, and only clicking links within the article body, use the clues provided by the Chrome Extension to navigate your way to Goose.
