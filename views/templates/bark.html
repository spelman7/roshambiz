A tree that fell in a forest which no one heard.

RoShamBark

$441or whatever is expensive to you.

RoShamBark hopes to solve an age-old philosophical question: If a tree falls in a forest with nobody there to hear it, does it make a sound? You get a block of wood, taken from an actual tree that fell in the woods with nobody around to hear it. The block is engraved with a QR code linking you to the sound of your specific tree falling. Did it make a sound? Your RoShamBark will tell you.

FAQ

Is this really made from a tree that fell in a forest that nobody heard?
Yep! We rigged up a system that let us fell a tree without actually being in the vicinity.
In those ads with Chris and “Cliff” Paul, if the two twin brothers were supposed to be separated at birth, why do they have the same last name?
We actually don't have an answer to this question. We are just overwhelmed and slightly saddened by the realization that we now live in a world where ads can have plot holes.
How did you get the tree to fall over?
A timer. A winch. A battery. A switch. Some vice grips. A lot of wire. And some really advanced audio recording equipment.
